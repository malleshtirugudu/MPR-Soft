MPR Software Services offers consulting and information technology (IT) services worldwide. Sevate operates primarily in three segments: IT services, Software products and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO). The IT Services segment provides a wide range of services, including software development, packaged software customization/integration, software maintenance, software design/engineering services. The Software products segment engages in the product development and creation of propriety software. The BPO segment provides services covering technical support, human resources, customer contact and transaction processing. The company offers services to customers in a range of industries, including insurance, banking and financial services, manufacturing, telecommunications, and transportation.MPR Soft offers & markets its services primarily to companies in the United States, and the Asia-Pacific region.

Systems Integration

The information your company generates is its most valuable asset. If you have multiple entry points for the same data, your data is stored in multiple locations or your data is generated from many different sources then you are at risk of having inaccurate or untimely information. MPR Soft's team of system integrators will evaluate your current information infrastructure and recommend solutions to consolidate your data.

This data consolidation will provide more accurate and timely information and will increase productivity by eliminating rework.
